BBD undertakes the retrofitting design as being a part of seismic vulneribility assessment. BBD also assists the clients to get approvals/ permissions from respective government departments.

MEP design services

MEP stands for “mechanical, electrical and plumbing.” MEP engineering is the science and art of planning, designing and managing the MEP systems of a building.

Architectural and interior design services

Simply put, an interior architect designs building interiors & space planning while an interior designer focuses on the actual furnishing and decoration of an interior

Design supervision

Interior design supervision process where designer should control implementation of all the tasks from design, choosing materials and workers

Fire safety assessment

A fire risk assessment is a review undertaken of a building in order to assess its fire risk and offer recommendations to make the building safer
